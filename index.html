About Me

Hello, I am Anissa Pooley. I live in Carlin, Nevada. I am a mom of five wonderful kids. I enjoy playing piano and singing. I also love to paint and draw. We love to go out as a family and explore the mountains of Nevada. We often explore ghost towns and find items left from the old mines. Of course, we have to be very careful and watch out for abandoned mine shafts. Most have been fenced off, but every now and then we find one that has been forgotten.

Most people associate Nevada with the dry, hot desert of Las Vegas. But, the area I live in is nearby the Humbolt River and surrounded by hills, mountains and vast open space.
